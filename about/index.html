TotalSoft History

  • 1994

    TOTALSOFT IS FOUNDED

    Operations begin in Bucharest, Romania, a crossroad between Europe, Middle-East and Asia.

  • 1998

    TOTALSOFT LAUNCHES HUMAN CAPITAL MANAGEMENT

    Need to keep track of your employees’ time? Want to hire new talent? Our software helps human resources managers get through the day.

  • 2002

    TOTALSOFT LAUNCHES CHARISMA ERP

    Are your taxes due? How will you fund the company’s growth? With our software, finance managers make better decisions.

  • 2004

    CHARISMA GOES INTERNATIONAL

    We helped La Fantana increase its bottled water production and distribution. The beverage company from Serbia also uses our software to talk to customers.

  • 2011

    TOTALSOFT AUSTRIA IS FOUNDED

    TotalSoft establishes a subsidiary in Vienna, Austria. TotalSoft Austria manages international sales and multi-country implementations of Charisma in leasing and microfinance companies.

  • 2016

    TOTALSOFT RECEIVES FOREIGN INVESTMENT

    Logo, a public company trading in Istanbul, acquires TotalSoft. Liviu Dragan remains as TotalSoft CEO in Bucharest.

  • 2016

    CHARISMA 4 RELEASE

    One solution. All business operations. Our software helps you manage finance, product cycles, employees, partners and customers.
