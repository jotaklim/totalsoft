Your personalized financial advice

Set your thermostat

In winter, heating can account for over 30% of your bill. Set your thermostat between 18 and 20 degrees. Every degree above 20 can add 10% to your heating bill. In summer, set your thermostat to 26 degrees or above.

Energy degree above 20 degrees can add 10% to your heating bill
Hire a professional to check your heating and cooling systems

Check and replace air filters regularly. Arrange for maintenance with qualified technicians at least once a year and avoid surprises.

Plant trees in front of your windows

Having a view to a garden that is well-cared-for will improve your mood and your energy efficiency. Shading trees help you save up to 15% in winter and up to 50% in summer.

Reduce lightning on low traffic areas

Reduce lightning near natural light sources and low traffic areas. Install motion detectors to automatically switch lights off in empty office spaces.

Use energy-efficient LCD monitors.

Use energy-efficient LCD monitors. Donate your CTR monitors to local schools and charities. By taking these actions you both help your local community and reduce your electricity bills.

Monitor when lights and devices are switched on.

Limit the hours that buildings are lit and climate-controlled during the business week. Also, ask your your security team to check for lights and devices that are switched on after business hours and educate staff in energy saving measures.

Switch computers, copy machines and printers off after business hours

Switching off computers, copy machines and printers off at night saves you up to 30% annually in energy costs.

Print smart

Replace stand-alone personal printers with multi-function systems. Increase energy efficiency and save toner and paper by defaulting to black and white, double-side printing.

Use heat rejection window films

Heat rejection window films allow maximum sunlight to enter the building, but block the heat. By using heat rejection window films you create a comfortable working environment and save up to 30% in energy.
