Your personalized financial advice

Set your thermostat

In winter, heating can account for over 30% of your bill. Set your thermostat between 18 and 20 degrees. Every degree above 20 can add 10% to your heating bill. In summer, set your thermostat to 26 degrees or above.

Temperature above 20 degrees can add 10% to your heating bill
Switch to ENERGY STAR devices

Using ENERGY STAR certified products throughout your home can save nearly 750 Euro over the lifetime of appliances, fans and electronic devices.

Reduce energy for water heating

Simple steps such as lowering your water-heater temperatures and choosing to wash clothes on cold water can bring your energy bills down significantly. Installing low-flow showerheads also helps.

Hire a professional to check your heating and cooling systems

Check and replace air filters regularly. Arrange for maintenance with qualified technicians at least once a year and avoid surprises.

Plant trees in front of your windows

Having a view to a garden that is well-cared-for will improve your mood and your energy efficiency.

Shading trees help you save up to 15% in winter and up to 50% in summer
Shift the time for completing household chores

The peak for energy consumption coincides with the higher rates per kWh. Shift the times you turn on the wash machine or vacuum the floor: complete your household chores in the morning or after 20:00.
